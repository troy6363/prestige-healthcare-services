Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Medicaid home care services, eligibility requirements, and professional caregivers in Shelby County.

What services does Prestige Healthcare Services, Inc. provide?

We provide non-medical home care services designed to help seniors and individuals with disabilities live safely and independently at home. Our core services include Personal Care (assistance with bathing, grooming, dressing, mobility, and medication reminders), Companion Services (companionship, light recreational activities, and running errands), and Homemaker Services (meal preparation, light housekeeping, laundry, and grocery shopping).

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve families throughout Shelby County, Tennessee, including Memphis, Bartlett, Germantown, Collierville, and surrounding communities.

Do you accept private pay or commercial health insurance?

No, Prestige Healthcare Services is a Medicaid-only provider. We exclusively accept Medicaid payments (such as TennCare CHOICES or related waiver programs) and do not accept private pay, credit cards, or commercial insurance at this time. This ensures our resources are dedicated fully to serving the Medicaid community.

How do I know if my loved one is eligible for Medicaid-covered home care?

If your loved one is enrolled in a Tennessee Medicaid program (like TennCare CHOICES) and meets the clinical and financial criteria for home care, their services may be 100% covered. We can assist you with verifying benefit eligibility and explaining the enrollment process during your free consultation.

Are your caregivers background-checked and trained?

Yes, absolutely. To ensure high care standards and safety, all caregivers undergo rigorous, state-compliant background checks, reference screenings, and interviews before entering homes. We also provide structured caregiver onboarding and continuous professional development.

How can I request a consultation or get started?

Getting started is simple. You can fill out our interactive Free Care Consultation Form on the home page, or call our toll-free line directly at 1-877-330-2012 (available 24 hours a day) or our local office at (901) 665-3131. A care coordinator will discuss your needs and help verify Medicaid coverage eligibility.

Still Have Questions or Ready to Begin?

Request a free consultation today, and our care team will contact you to review your loved one's needs and help verify Medicaid benefit coverage.
