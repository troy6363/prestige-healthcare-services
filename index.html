About Us

The Heart Behind Prestige Healthcare Services

Our Mission

To bridge care gaps with respect, compassion, and dignity.

Prestige Healthcare Services was founded from a lifelong passion for serving others and helping individuals maintain their dignity, independence, and quality of life. After more than 24 years of working in education—supporting children, families, seniors, and individuals with disabilities—our founder recognized a growing need for compassionate, dependable, and personalized care services within our local communities.

Throughout this career, working closely with individuals facing physical, emotional, developmental, and life challenges reinforced a fundamental truth: supportive care must not only meet practical needs, but also promote genuine human connection and respect.

Prestige Healthcare Services was created to bridge this gap. We offer premium, high-quality non-medical home care services that allow individuals to remain safely and comfortably in their own homes while receiving the support they need.

Payment Structure

Coverage Information

Medicaid Provider Only

Prestige Healthcare Services, Inc. currently accepts Medicaid payments exclusively for all non-medical home care services. We do not accept private pay or commercial insurances at this time.

If you or your loved one are enrolled in a Tennessee Medicaid program (such as TennCare CHOICES or related waiver programs), your care services may be 100% covered. We can assist you with verifying benefit eligibility during your consultation.

Who We Assist:

  • Seniors seeking to age safely and comfortably in place
  • Family caregivers in need of essential respite support
  • Veterans requiring support with activities of daily living
  • Adults with developmental or physical disabilities
  • Anyone needing dedicated non-medical household support
